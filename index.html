Most Mind-blowing movie

Released in 2010


Plot. In 1954, U.S. Marshals Edward "Teddy" Daniels and his new partner Chuck Aule travel to the Ashecliffe Hospital for the criminally insane on Castle Island in Boston Harbor. They are investigating the disappearance of patient Rachel Solando, incarcerated for drowning her three children.



Directed by: Martin Scorsese

Genre: Neo-noir, Psychological thriller
